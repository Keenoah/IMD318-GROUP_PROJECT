Kitchen's Story

From Me to You, For All of Us.

Welcome to Samuta Restaurant, where togetherness begins.

Reserve Now! Say Hi

SAMUTA- Saya, Kamu, Kita nestled in the heart of Sungai Petani, our restaurant is more than just a place to grab a bite—it’s a space where dreams, passion, and community come together. Officially opening its doors in late 2024, the café represents a journey that started with a shared love for food, great coffee, and creating a welcoming atmosphere.

The idea for the restaurant came during one of those late-night conversations among friends, where dreams feel possible, and ideas come alive. We wanted to build something that wasn’t just another eatery, but a space that felt like home—a spot where people could unwind, share stories, and savor every bite of something made with love.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create a space where every individual feels at home, fostering a community connected by the love of food and togetherness.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, high-quality food in an environment that celebrates unity, culture, and shared experiences.
